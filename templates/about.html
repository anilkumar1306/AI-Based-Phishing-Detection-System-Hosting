About Our Project

Our AI-Based Phishing Detection System is designed to identify phishing emails using advanced machine learning models. The system analyzes email content for suspicious patterns, links, and language that might indicate a phishing attempt, providing an additional layer of security for users.

Technologies Used

  • Models
    LSTM, CNN, RNN
  • Packages
    PyTorch, TensorFlow, Flask
  • Libraries
    NumPy, Pandas

How It Works

Our system extracts features from email content including text, headers, and embedded URLs. These features are then processed through our trained AI models to predict whether the email is legitimate or a phishing attempt. The multiple models work together to provide comprehensive detection capabilities with high accuracy rates.
